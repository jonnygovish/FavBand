Who are

Sauti Sol?

Sauti Sol are a Kenyan afro-pop band formed in Nairobi, Kenya by vocalists Bien-Aimé Baraza, Willis Chimano, Savara Mudigi and Polycarp Otieno in 2005.

  

It initially started as an a capella group.After frequent interaction and interest in diversifying to professional music, they formed the band Sauti.The music group later added, Sol to their name, making their full name Sauti Sol (Voices of the Sun).

  

In 2006, the band participated in the Spotlight on Kenyan Music competition, hosted by the Alliance française in Nairobi. They were included in the album compilation recording and later got signed to Penya Records. Sauti Sol released their debut studio album Mwanzo on 4 August 2009, via their record label Penya Records, to critical acclaim, and followed up with their second studio album Sol Filosofia two years later

Meet the Members

Bien-Aime Baraza

Vocalist, songwriter and guitarist

  

Bien-Aime (French for beloved) Baraza is a vocalist, songwriter and guitarist. His style of singing is soulful and his songwriting skills celebrated by many.

Willis Austin Chimano

Vocalist, keytar and saxophone player

  

Willis Austin Chimano is a vocalist, keytar and saxophone player. His deep voice and flamboyant style makes him stand out in the group. Before the formation of Sauti Sol, Chimano contested and won in the annual Fête de la Musique music competition at the French Cultural Centre

Polycarp Otieno

Guitarist and producer

  

Polycarp Otieno is a guitarist and producer. His signature style – guitar riffs blends with the group’s mix of soulful vocal harmonies and drum rhythm. His love for the guitar started early when he attended free guitar lessons at All Saints Cathedral.

Savara Mudigi

vocalist, percussionist, drummer and producer

  

Savara Mudigi is a vocalist, percussionist, drummer and producer. His versatility in the band has played an instrumental role during tour and in the establishment on their imprint label: Sauti Sol Entertainment.
